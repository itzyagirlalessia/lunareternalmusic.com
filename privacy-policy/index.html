Privacy Policy

How Lunar Eternal Music Group handles your personal data.

Last updated: July 2026

Who We Are

Lunar Eternal Music Group is an independent music label based in the Netherlands. We operate the website at lunareternalmusic.com.

For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at info@lunareternalmusic.com.

What Data We Collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide to us. This includes:

  • Your name and email address when you contact us via email
  • Any information you include in remix contest submissions
  • Any information you provide when signing up for updates or notifications

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics tools on this website. We do not collect data passively.

How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data solely for the purpose for which you provided it:

  • To respond to your enquiries or messages
  • To process and evaluate remix contest submissions
  • To send you updates you have explicitly requested

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

We are based in the Netherlands and operate under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). The legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent — when you contact us or submit information voluntarily
  • Legitimate interest — when responding to business enquiries

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. Email correspondence is kept for a maximum of 2 years unless there is an ongoing business relationship that requires longer retention.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to correct inaccurate data
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@lunareternalmusic.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Third-Party Services

Our website links to third-party platforms including Spotify, YouTube, and Instagram. These platforms have their own privacy policies and we are not responsible for their data practices.

Our website is hosted via GitHub Pages. GitHub's own privacy policy applies to the hosting infrastructure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The most recent version will always be available at this page. We will notify you of significant changes via email where applicable.

Contact

For any questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at info@lunareternalmusic.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens) at autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl.
